Nate Tharp is a visual designer that has been creating websites, building experiences and developing brands for over a decade.

He publishes curated Internet goodness in his Notebook, drops knowledge on Twitter 140 characters at a time, sporadicly posts snapshots to Flickr, and catalogs his material wants and desires on Svpply.

Born in the Midwest, raised in New England, and educated in the South, Nate now makes his home in Brooklyn, NY.

Yes, he does play basketball. That is in fact his natural eye color. He is an Eagle Scout. At the age of five he won a giant stuffed bear in a drug-store raffle. And in 2008 he coined the term "fashhole."
